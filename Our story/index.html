THIS IS OUR STORY

SpotOn Apparel is story of two resilient women (Founders of SpotOn Apparel) who decided to put an end to the unfair trade practices having been at the receiving end of such practices where they kept waiting for their ordered Kid`s customized sports apparel having finally received them long after the tournament was over and finding the received goods to be of an inferior quality with multiple design / customization errors.

Further “Research” led them to unearth millions of fellow Americans being taken for a ride by such, much publicized and advertised companies (a few even claimed to be “Revolutionizing sports” LMAO!) Many companies were found to be “Quick” in taking payments with some really awesome sales services which just vanished the moment a customer paid. The customers were left at their own mercies and had no alternative then to either get their apparel locally or just sit on the side-lines since they didn’t have uniforms to play in. After closely watching customers finding local guys for last minute fix, complaining about incorrect colours, poor designs, wrong sizes, lack of after sales support and most importantly delay in delivery, The Founders then took it upon themselves to put an end to this long going adversity.

The huge market and immense potential of customised sports apparel business can be brought to justice only with someone more humane and overtly sincere in doing business.

Being already experienced businesswomen and existing network in Asia (SpotOn Fashionwear) didn’t take them much time to launch their own Customized Sports Apparel Brand and SpotOn Apparel was thus born. Any vacuum of experience and practical implementation of such a business was plugged by hiring a man with 15 years’ experience dealing in Custom Sports Apparel business who will head the Sales and Operations as our V.P. Ops with Spot On Apparel and Spot On Group of Companies.
